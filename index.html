Meet My Groomsmen

Long before I met the woman I would marry I met the friends (Freddie, Tyler, Lamorne, Angelo & Justin) that would be by my side on that day. These guys and I have been through good times, bad times, and countless crazy times, but we’ve (surprisingly) survived it all. When it came to choosing groomsmen there was no doubt these six will be standing up there with me on November 24, 2017.

Freddie - My Best Man

Freddie has always been and will always be my favorite beer pong partner. He cares a lot about his appearance and fancies himself a Drake lookalike. Some of his favorite things include mirror selfies and snapchats of his car. There are very few people in the world that know less about sports than Freddie but some how even with all these terrible traits he is still my best friend and my best man.

Tyler - Groomsman

Tyler is mean, witty, opinionated, and gossips like a teenage girl. There is no way to avoid laughing at his jokes, even when you are the punchline. He’s a person you want on your side in a fight, believe me, I’ve seen him lift an enormous dumpster. Most importantly Tyler can outdrink anyone I know but please never give him Jäger.

Lamorne - Groomsman

When Lamorne is around you are sure to have a good time. He’s the life of every party, whether he’s dancing or telling one of the most elaborate stories you’ve ever heard he’s making the whole room laugh.

Angelo - Groomsman

Angelo and I met in elementary school making him one of my oldest friends. We went from playing at recess together to being in the same graduating high school class. If I ever need help with anything I know I can call him and he will be there with a smile asking what he can do. He is hands down the nicest of my friends and anyone who knows him would say that same thing.

Justin - Groomsman

Justin and I grew up in the same neighborhood. We went from playing sports outside all day to video games all night. As a teenager I spent more time at his house than my own, his family became mine. Years have passed but whenever we get together it is like old times all over again.
